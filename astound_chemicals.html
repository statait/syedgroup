Astound Chemical

Astound Chemical Industries is a proud concern of the esteemed Syed Group of Industries, embodying a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and excellence. We specialize in producing high-quality Sodium Silicate through a groundbreaking, environmentally friendly process utilizing rice husk ash.


Our eco-conscious manufacturing approach not only reduces industrial waste but also significantly lowers our carbon footprint, contributing to a greener and more sustainable future. As part of the Syed Group of Industries, we uphold the legacy of delivering reliable, top-tier solutions that align with the global demand for sustainable industrial practices.


Astound Chemical Industries is your trusted partner for premium-quality, environmentally responsible Sodium Silicate products.
