Bangladesh’s first ever industrial/home automation company: STATA IT Limited

Smart Home Automation is one of the boons of the 21st century that promises not only luxury and comfort but also security and viability

2021 is the year that has been termed by millennials as the year of flying cars, talking robots, and whatnot. Although the world could not meet the above promises, it did excel in making machines and smart devices talk to each other. Smart Home Automation is one of the boons of the 21st century that promises not only luxury and comfort, but also security and viability, long after its inception in 2014 through the hands of Vodafone and their M2M (Machine to Machine) protocol for Internet Of Things (IOT) devices. The IOT technology enables pieces of technology to communicate with each other using a wide range of communication pathways on the internet, meaning every device connected to the internet can communicate with each other, as long as they have the same credentials.



In order to keep up with the world of technology, a Bangladesh based start-up has finally cracked the secret code to Smart Home Automation. Stata IT Limited is the first IT Company to take the bold initiative to introduce Smart Home/Industrial Automation devices and a Proprietary App: StataLife. Smart home devices vary from Smart Gang Switches to Smart Door Locks. The devices use a proprietary processor that communicates only with the Smartphone App, allowing potential access to more than 1000+ STATA products from anywhere around the globe. Stata IT Limited started its operation back in 2018, but has gone into a fully functional revenue model from 2020, upon launching Bangladesh’s First Smart Home APP.

Some of the main attractions of Stata IT limited are: Smart Wi-Fi Switches, Smart Fan Regulators, Smart Motion Sensing Camera, Smart Doorbell with 2X Zoom Camera, and other smart home peripherals.



Additionally, the STATA UIR is a smart Infra-Red sensor that can allow control of more than 200+ IR controlled devices using only the StataLife app. Along with the UIR, STATA has introduced a Smart Cleaning Bot that can map one’s house completely and automatically navigate around the house to clean all the interiors with its powerful suction and wiping.

In the year 2020 alone, STATA IT Limited has served and automated over 550 consumer homes and created a large distribution hub around Dhaka, with over 15 distribution outlets. The company is not limited to the mega city; it has also seen its inception in the Port City of Chattogram. The company further wishes to arrange IOT Automation based Workshops, Webinars and Socio-Economic Gatherings to inspire the young generation of Bangladesh towards IT and automation. STATA IT Limited believes that one day, The Digital Bangladesh Dream will be successful, and wishes to be Bangladesh’s First Smart Home Automation and Robotics Manufacturer.
